# Assumptions

The following assumptions underpin this proposal. Stakeholders should review and confirm each before acting on any recommendation.

1. **No internal Campari data access** — All content is based on publicly available regulatory information and BroadBrand's independent research. No proprietary Campari systems, datasets, or internal documentation were consulted.

2. **Directional strategy only** — The recommendations in this proposal are strategic guidance, not legal advice. They are intended to inform decision-making, not replace professional legal counsel.

3. **Local legal validation required** — All market-specific guidance must be validated with qualified local legal counsel before activation in any market.

4. **Platform policies subject to change** — Social media and advertising platform policies change frequently. Verify current platform rules and restrictions before activating any campaign.

5. **Regulatory landscape as of March 2026** — The regulatory information presented reflects the landscape assessed as of March 2026. Subsequent legislative or regulatory changes may affect applicability.
